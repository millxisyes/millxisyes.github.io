    Millx's Favorite Games

    Persona 5 Royal

    Why?
    Persona 5 Royal is an extremely fun JRPG with incredible world building. The vibe of the game is wonderful, the UI is extremely nice.
    Persona 5 Royal is a game about a team of high schoolers, who awaken to an ability called a "Persona". Persona's are basically these entities, which have powers, and are a representation of the person's true self. The high schoolers find out about a place called a "Palace", which is how a person truly sees a place, these palaces are filled with enemies, and they require Persona's to be beaten. The high schoolers find out that their gym teacher is a terrible person, who abuses their students, so they change his heart, by going into the person's palace, and defeating them. This creates a change of heart, and prevents them from doing anymore evil activities. This game has wonderful story, character building and characters, with a brilliant mix between a social simulator and a JRPG. The amount of absurd plot twists is absolutely mind-boggling, and this game is a must play for those looking for story-driven games.    10/10, still playing, but is absolutely amazing, and has become my favourite game.

    Persona 4 Golden

    Why?
    Persona 4 Golden is an extremely well written narrative, with the main theme being around friendship. In the beginning of the game, you move into a small town called Inaba. Around the time you moved into Inaba, a serial killing case starts, as you and your friends that you make in Inaba, attempt to find a culprit. You and your friends go through intense moments, of sadness, anger and happiness, in this wonderfully crafted narrative.    10/10, second best game I've ever played.

    Danganronpa 2: Goodbye Despair

    Why?
    Danganronpa 2 is an extremely well written narrative where sixteen students come to an island for a class trip, but they don't have any memories regarding their school. They find out that a bear named Monokuma who has taken over the island and installed surveillance cameras, and also adds a new rule to the island. You must kill someone, and get away with it to leave the island, and if you are caught, then you will be executed. If you aren't caught, everyone but you will be executed. Danganronpa 2 is a game of constantly doubting others, and anticipating others moves, while also keeping track of clues from murders, so you can find out the culprit. This game is absolutely wild with tons of plot twist, and amazing atmosphere which makes Danganronpa 2: Goodbye Despair in my Top 3 best games of all time.    10/10, peak.

    Favorite Music/Song from the OST:

    Danganronpa: Trigger Happy Havoc

    Why?
    Danganronpa: Trigger Happy Havoc is the first game in the Danganronpa series, and is absolutely incredible. The game starts off with the protagonist, Makoto Naegi winning a lottery, and being able to enter the most prestigious high school in Japan, called Hope's Peak Academy. Hope's Peak Academy is where the best of the best students attend. When Makoto enters Hope's Peak Academy, his vision warps as he falls unconsciousness, and wakes up in a classroom. All the windows are covered and there are surveillance cameras in the classroom. He finds out that he needs to enter the gym, where a bear named "Monokuma" tells them that they're trapped in Hope's Peak Academy, and to leave, you must kill someone and get away with it. This game's wonderful atmosphere alongside insane plot twists, and crazy murders, makes it my fourth favorite game of all time.    10/10, absolutely amazing.

    Doki Doki Literature Club

    Why?
    Doki Doki Literature Club hides itself as a dating sim romance visual novel, but quickly turns into one of the most horrifying psychological horror visual novels. This game had me gasping for air when I first played it, with insane revelations and surprises. Although this game is a measly two hours, it's a great experience for those looking to be scared shitless.    10/10, absolutely peak.
